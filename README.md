# EverRail Wiki

Independent English guide hub for **EverRail** at
`https://www.everrail.wiki`.

The launch site covers:

- a practical first-run loop for the train and its expeditions;
- battery energy, passengers, rations, wagons, storage and upgrades;
- glider scouting, ISO, crafting, recycling, cold and hunger;
- combat, robots, EMP tools and up-to-four-player co-op roles;
- all 33 official Steam achievements;
- Early Access context, system requirements, review and safe Steam download;
- WebSite, Organization, VideoGame, VideoObject, FAQPage, ItemList, Article and
  BreadcrumbList structured data.

## Source policy

- The official Steam App 4134600 page and Aesir Interactive are the primary
  identity and release sources.
- Early Access systems can change, so live-state claims are dated and checked
  against official sources.
- Community strategies are labeled as guidance rather than official rules.
- No fake codes, unsupported tier lists, cracks, APK mirrors, trainers or
  extracted game files.

EverRail and official materials belong to their respective rights holders.
