Checked: 2026-07-21

## Exact-game result set

No mature dedicated EverRail wiki or guide domain was visible in the current English result set one day after Early Access launch. Exact-game results are led by the official Steam store/community, launch-day YouTube gameplay, store databases and a co-op information page.

| Site | Relevant route model | Main lesson |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Steam Store / Community | Official entity, announcements, achievements and discussions | Primary source for live identity, four-player limit, achievements, requirements and changing Early Access systems |
| Aesir Interactive | Official game overview and media | Strongest trust source for train, glider, expedition and co-op pillars |
| Co-Optimus | One direct EverRail co-op profile | Exact co-op intent exists, but the page is not a complete guide hub |
| YouTube launch results | Beginner, day-one, solo, co-op and developer streams | Current player interest clusters around first-run decisions and system understanding |

## Opportunity

- Launch with a compact English hub and 15 supported intent pages.
- Lead with train battery, beginner, passengers/rations, expeditions, co-op and achievements.
- Keep every Early Access value source-checked and dated.
- Do not publish codes, tier lists, a static map or a copied Voidtrain-style database.
- Expand from GSC evidence after the first crawl rather than guessing hundreds of entities.

Full keyword, source, schema, breadth and implementation decisions are recorded in `docs/everrail-wiki-prd-and-implementation-plan.md`.
